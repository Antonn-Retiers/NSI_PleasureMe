The Advocaat

Advocaat, a distinguished liqueur of Dutch origin, offers a refined indulgence for those with a penchant for the finer things in life. With its velvety texture and harmonious blend of aged brandy, fresh eggs, sugar, and vanilla, this luxurious beverage has been a symbol of opulence since its inception in the 17th century. Not to be confused with the more common eggnog, Advocaat is revered for its rich consistency and its sophisticated balance of sweetness and brandy warmth. Its delightful viscosity and gentle complexity make it the ideal companion for those seeking a smooth, sumptuous drink that elevates any occasion. Traditionally enjoyed neat or over ice, Advocaat is also a remarkable ingredient for crafting sophisticated cocktails or as a decadent addition to desserts. Its allure extends beyond mere consumption; it serves as a versatile companion to a fine meal or a delightful after-dinner treat, often accompanying artisanal pastries or delicate confections. For the discerning connoisseur, Advocaat offers an unparalleled sensory experience - a testament to craftsmanship and heritage, perfect for those who appreciate the subtle elegance of a well-crafted libation.
Warning! Alcohol abuse harms your health!

•1 large egg
•30g (2 tablespoons) granulated sugar
•45ml brandy
•1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
•A pinch of salt

Instructions



Prepare the Base:
In a mixing bowl, whisk the egg and sugar together until the mixture becomes smooth and slightly frothy. This will form the base of your Advocaat.

Infuse the Flavors:
Add the brandy (or your preferred mix of brandy and neutral spirit) to the egg-sugar mixture, along with the vanilla extract. Stir gently to combine. If using vanilla bean-infused brandy, the flavor will be richer and more aromatic.

Optional Touches:
For added depth, include a pinch of salt. You may also infuse your brandy with spices, such as cinnamon or a few cardamom pods, to add complexity.

Chill (Optional):
For a slightly cooler version, you can chill the mixture in the refrigerator for 30 minutes or serve immediately, depending on preference.

Serve with Elegance:
Pour the mixture into a fine glass, such as a small coupe or cordial glass.

Garnish:
Add a dollop of freshly whipped cream on top of the Advocaat. For extra luxury, lightly dust with freshly grated nutmeg or cinnamon. For an extra decadent touch, garnish with edible gold leaf, grated dark chocolate, or a small twist of orange zest.



Enjoy:
Sip slowly and savor the creamy, luxurious texture of this elevated Advocaat. Ideal as an after-dinner indulgence or a special treat for a refined occasion.
